November 15, 2024

 


Dear Benjamin,

Thank you for applying to the University at Buffalo, New York's flagship. We truly appreciate the time and effort you put into your application.
 
  After careful consideration of an exceptionally competitive pool of applicants, we regret to inform you that we are unable to offer you admission to UB for the Fall 2025 semester. This decision was not an easy one and reflects the high level of academic achievement and talent demonstrated by all candidates this year. 
 
Although this may not be the decision you were hoping for, we encourage you to continue striving toward your goals and pursuing the path that will lead to your future successes.

Please know that this decision is not a reflection of your potential but rather the result of limited space and an extraordinarily competitive applicant pool.

We wish you the very best as you move forward and hope you find a college or university that aligns with your ambitions and aspirations.

Sincerely,


                                                                       
Ebony L. Dixon
Associate Vice Provost
Director of Admissions and Enrollment 
University at Buffalo

 *All decisions are provisional upon receipt and validation of courses and grades on the official final high school transcript.
