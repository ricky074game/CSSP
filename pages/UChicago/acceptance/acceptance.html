November 29, 2024

| The University of Chicago College Admissions

2024-11-29T17:00:00

Benjamin Rudinski

Dear Benjamin,

It is my great pleasure to inform you that you have been admitted to the University of Chicago's Class of 2029. With your exceptional academic record, strong voice, and impressive accomplishments, you have distinguished yourself in one of the largest and most competitive applicant pools in our history.

For over a century, the College's academic tradition has thrived because of students like you who crave ideas that captivate, provoke, and transform. We imagine you in our laboratories and on our performance stages, in our libraries and on our athletic fields, immersed in your passions and engaged in the pursuit of knowledge. As part of our College Houses, you will cultivate lifelong friendships; in our classrooms, you will create and inhabit a vibrant intellectual space with your peers and professors.

In the coming weeks, I invite you to explore all that UChicago has to offer. Join us for one of our Admitted Student Open Houses in April, where you can experience firsthand our rich community of scholars, friends, and neighbors. You will receive additional information about arranging your visit and confirming your enrollment at UChicago in your Applicant Portal.

To secure your place in the Class of 2029, please submit your enrollment deposit by May 1, 2025. We look forward to receiving your reply as soon as you are ready.

We invite you to be part of a school with a glorious history and an exciting present. We look to you to help us grow, to grow with us, and to be part of a tradition that elevates us all.

Sincerely,

James G. Nondorf
Dean of Admissions and Financial Aid
