November 20, 2024

Dear Benjamin,

Thank you for applying to Binghamton University’s Harpur College of Arts and Sciences for Fall 2025. We appreciate the time and effort you put into your application and the opportunity to learn more about you.

After careful consideration, we regret to inform you that we are unable to offer you admission at this time. This year, we received a record number of applications from exceptionally talented and accomplished students, making our decisions especially challenging.

Please know that this decision does not reflect your potential or the impressive achievements you’ve demonstrated. We encourage you to continue pursuing your academic and personal goals with the same dedication and commitment that brought you to this point.

We wish you all the best in your future endeavors and hope you find success and fulfillment in the opportunities ahead.

Best,


Krista Medionte-Phillips
Assistant Provost for Enrollment Management
Director of Undergraduate Admissions
